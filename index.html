The Strory

Amid Agrigo ,Derrick and Anne M.CAuther were tasked with exploring untouched parts of Kenya by Kenyan Government with the aim of providing strategic plan to improve the life style of kenyans, but they found a more urget and serious case that need solving with immediate actions so they turn to Government hepl but as it is well known Government help takes time it must first be presented to partliement then passed by the MPS yet they had no time so they to it upone them self to tackle and the bull by the horns and make sure that no child in Kenya would sleep hunger

The Technology behide our work

Many ask how we locate people that need our help,Well we use an algorithim developed by our software team to

  • Scan trending post
  • Locate most need towns, counties and villages
  • Most mention area
  • Areas prone to disasters
  • Collaborate with Government
  • Collaborate with NGOs
  • Ads to adverties our work
  • Ralies and Social media posts
